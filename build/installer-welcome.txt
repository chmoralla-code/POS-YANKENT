YANKENT POS
Construction & Supply Point of Sale
Version 1.0

Welcome to YANKENT POS - the offline-first point of sale system designed
for construction supply and hardware stores.

WHAT'S INCLUDED:
  - Full POS application with product catalog, sales, and reporting
  - Thermal printer driver (POS-58) - pre-installed to your Downloads
  - Telegram report integration: send sales summaries to the owner
  - Automatic backup every 5 minutes
  - Restock history tracking
  - VAT-exclusive pricing with configurable rate

GETTING STARTED:
  1. Install and launch YANKENT POS
  2. Log in with: admin / admin123
  3. Change the admin password immediately (Settings > Users)
  4. Configure your store name, address, and TIN (Settings > Store Info)
  5. Add your products and set prices (Products tab)
  6. Start selling (Point of Sale tab)

DEFAULT LOGIN:
  Username: admin
  Password: admin123

IMPORTANT: Change the default password on first login.
